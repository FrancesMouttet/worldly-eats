LONDON

NAC

Hands down the best dessert in London. It’s a warm chocolate chip cookie served with cornflake cereal ice cream and topped with even more cereal.

It sounds a little chaotic, but it works. It’s easily the best cookie dessert I’ve ever had. When I lived in London for a year, my sisters and I went every two weeks like clockwork.

Ambiance: 9/10    Service: 8/10    Food: 10/10    Value: 9/10    Overall: 9/10

Yorkshire Burrito

Sunday roasts are one of my favorite meals, so when my sister and I went to Camden Market just to walk around, I was immediately drawn to this stand. It was a full Sunday roast wrapped inside a giant Yorkshire pudding, basically a burrito, but British.

The second I took a bite, I didn’t move. I’m not even sure I breathed. I ate it so fast because it was that good. All the textures and flavors came together perfectly: gravy, meat, potato, everything.

And no, I did not let my sister try it. Not even a bite.

Ambiance: 10/10    Service: 10/10    Food: 10/10    Value: 10/10    Overall: 10/10
