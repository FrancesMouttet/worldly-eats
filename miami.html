MIAMI

LPM Restaurant & Bar

The first time I went here was in 2022 for my 21st birthday with my parents, and I still think about it constantly. It was also the first time I ever tried escargot, and I loved it so much I made them order a second round just for me.

We had a perfectly cooked medium steak, paired with a pomegranate corn salad that was absolutely insane, the perfect balance of sweet and tangy. We also ordered garlic green beans that I expected to be hot, but they came cold and were somehow even better, fresh, bright, and ridiculously flavorful. The roasted broccolini was another perfect add-on.

But the real star? The crème brûlée. This is what they’re known for, and now I understand why. It was a flawless 10/10, the best I’ve ever had. The texture was silky, the sugar crust was perfectly caramelized, and you could taste the fresh vanilla bean in every bite. Not too big, not too small, just perfect.

Ambiance: 9/10    Service: 8/10    Food: 10/10    Value: 9/10    Overall: 9/10
